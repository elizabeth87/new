About Me

Story goes after a few months of coding and perseverance you become an expert in the field. Well... at least that's what I read in someones overly optimistic blog. I on the other hand believe that not only perseverance but failing a thousand times will get you closer to your goal because it clears the path. Failing pushes you beyond measure to keep persevering towards the end goal. I don't seek to become an expert but I do seek to push myself hard enough to become skilled and confident in what I do. So follow me on my journey in becoming a skilled computer programmer. I started this site so people like me who have worked hard and persevered but still feel far from an expert can come and share their share of failings to sort out whats needed to become adept in this every changing tech world.
I'am a drifter by heart anchoring now in the world of tech...
